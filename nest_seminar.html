Network Stochastic Processes and Time Series Seminar

The NeST seminars are a series of talks, followed by informal discussions, on topics relating the study of large dynamic networks and the themes covered in the NeST programme. Each seminar is 60 minutes long and takes place virtually, on Zoom. A link to join will be posted here shortly before each seminar.

The seminar is currently organised by Alexander Modell. For any questions, please email a.modell@imperial.ac.uk.
