Research

I am a PhD student at the Centre for Doctoral Training in Computational Statistics and Data Science, University of Bristol. I am supervised by Dr. Patrick Rubin-Delanchy.

My research is about developing and understanding statistical methodology for finding patterns and structure in large-scale networks and detecting anomalies in complex datasets.

In an ongoing collaboration, this methodology is being used to detect signs of corruption in procurement data from Latin America and the Caribbean as part of the Microsoft ACTS (Anti-Corruption Technologies and Solutions) initiative.
