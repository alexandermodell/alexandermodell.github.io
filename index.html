Research

I am a PhD student at the Centre for Doctoral Training in Computational Statistics and Data Science, University of Bristol. I am supervised by Dr. Patrick Rubin-Delanchy.

My research is about finding patterns and structure in large-scale networks and detecting anomalies in complex datasets, primarily driven by cyber-security applications.
